## Current Research

<article class="feature-card">
  <header class="feature-card__meta">
    <span class="badge badge--accent">Active</span>
    <span class="badge">Ongoing</span>
  </header>
  <h3 class="feature-card__title">Privacy-Preserving LLM Inference under FHE</h3>
  <p class="feature-card__role"><strong>Principal Investigator</strong></p>
  <div class="feature-card__body">
    <p>Making large language model inference run under fully homomorphic encryption with practical latency - bringing private LLM serving within reach of real-world deployments.</p>
    <ul>
      <li>FHE-friendly approximations of transformer building blocks (softmax, GELU, layer norm).</li>
      <li>Bootstrapping schedules and packing strategies tailored to attention and MLP layers.</li>
      <li>End-to-end latency and accuracy evaluation against plaintext baselines.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</article>
